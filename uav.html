Quad Copter for Organ transfer

The system was designed to carry a payload of 2 kgs (ideal scenario of organ transfer). The prototype was made using aluminium and equivalent motor, propeller and esc was chosen. After programming and fine tuning PID values, we were able to achieve 90% stability of commercial drones. Choice of material and aerodynamic stability were the crucial part of the design.
